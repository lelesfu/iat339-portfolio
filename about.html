ABOUT ME

Make everyday design with curious mind and bold heart.

  • UX Design
  • WEB design
  • Filmmaking

My name is Tao Liu, an UX designer with a curious mind. I’m currently a fourth year SFU student majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology in Vancouver BC, who tackles challenges with interaction design, visual design, and strategic thinking.


My skills include Filmmaking (Sound, Editing, Cinematography etc.) Programming (html/css, JavaScript, Java, C, C#), Writing, and Game Development.


My strong fascination with human behaviour helps me facilitate better interactions through meaningful and delightful designs. I value learning and continuously push myself to take on new opportunities so I can grow as a person, as well as a designer.


The web is currently the most powerful factor in social structures, commerce and business interactions and it is vital to have the systems in place to facilitate those interactions. With not only front end web skills but also backend knowledge, I really hope to follow a career path that puts me on the cutting edge of those systems, to both build and maintain them.
